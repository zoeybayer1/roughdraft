The prices of the physical art depend on the medium

and how big the piece is. Sketches and smaller

pieces will be cheaper than bigger fully lined

pieces. Keep in mind that delivery will affect how

quickly you recieve the artwork.

The prices of the digital art pieces are also cheaper

if it is sketches rather than lined or color pieces.

Although, the digital art differs from the physical

in that you will recieve the piece a lot faster

as there is no delivery time.
